Our Storm & Flood Damage Repair Service Process

#1 Inspection & Assessment

#1 Inspection & Assessment

Upon arriving at the worksite, the first task is to properly assess the scope of damage of each square foot, any complicating factors or safety risks, and identify the types of water damage.

 

Since not all the flood damage or infiltration is immediately apparent, We use specialized tools such as infrared cameras or moisture meters to detect even the most hidden patches of water within your property.

#2 Water Extraction & Drying

The next step is to remove any water that could cause damage and control mold growth to avoid mold problems in the areas exposed to mold spores.

 

We use water extraction units such as truck-mounted water extractors, submersible pumps, and water vacuums to remove water from surfaces.

 

Drying equipment is then installed to remove lingering moisture from the area. We document every detail of the storm damage repairs for flood insurance purposes.

#3 Make Insurance Claims

The stress of dealing with storm damage is alleviated by having a storm insurance policy. We assist you in completing your claim and then scheduling an on-site visit of your property with an insurance company representative.

 

Our licensed technicians help you with the documentation of the damage and restoration so that your insurance claim gets processed more quickly. In addition, we work with several insurance companies, which makes processing claims much more accessible.
